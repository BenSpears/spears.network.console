Thank you for playing **Find Four**. Your privacy matters, and this app is
built to respect it. This policy explains what information the app does and
does not handle.

## The short version

**Find Four does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
information.** The app works entirely offline, requires no account, and asks
for no permissions.

## Information we collect

**None.** Find Four does not collect any personal data or usage data. The app
contains:

- No analytics or tracking tools.
- No advertising or ad networks.
- No third-party SDKs.
- No crash-reporting services.
- No login, account, or registration system.

## Network access

Find Four does **not** connect to the internet. It makes no network requests
and sends no data to us or to any third party. All gameplay, audio, and
graphics are generated and run entirely on your device.

## Data stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of information **locally on your device only**, so
your preferences and progress are remembered between sessions. This includes:

- Your selected difficulty.
- Your level progress for each difficulty.
- The total number of four-leaf clovers you have found.
- Your chosen background sound and sound-effect preferences.

This information never leaves your device, is never transmitted to us, and is
not accessible to anyone else. It is removed if you delete the app.

## Permissions

Find Four requests **no special permissions**. It does not access your
location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, calendar, health data,
Bluetooth, or any other sensitive feature of your device. All audio is
synthesized by the app and played through the standard audio system; the
microphone is never used.

## Children's privacy

Because Find Four collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all
ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including
children under the age of 13.

## Third parties

Find Four does not include any third-party software, services, or advertising.
No data is shared with third parties because no data is collected.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on
this page with a new "Last updated" date. Since the app collects no data, any
changes are expected to be minor (for example, clarifications or contact
updates).
