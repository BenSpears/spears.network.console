# Read Forge Privacy Policy

*Effective July 13, 2026*

Read Forge is built to read text, not to collect it. The short version:
**Read Forge collects no data about you. None leaves your device to me or to
anyone else.**

## Data collection

Read Forge has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of
any kind. The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal
information or usage data.

## Your reading content

Text you paste, files you import, and articles you share into Read Forge are
stored only on your device. Your library and reading positions never leave it.
Deleting the app deletes all of this data.

## Sharing text and articles into Read Forge

When you use *Copy to Read Forge* from the share sheet, the shared text or link
is passed directly from the share extension to the app through private,
on-device storage that only Read Forge can access.

## Fetching articles

When you share a web link, Read Forge downloads that page directly from the
website to your device to extract its text — the same way a browser would. That
request goes only to the website you chose; no intermediary server is involved,
and Read Forge keeps no record of the sites you read. The websites you visit
may log the request per their own privacy policies, as they would for any
visit.

## Children

Read Forge does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including
children.
